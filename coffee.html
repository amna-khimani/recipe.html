Cold Coffee

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 tsp coffee crystal
  • sweetener of choice(optional)
  • 2 cup of hot water
  • Ice
  • milk or creamer of choice (optional)

Method:

  1. Fill a 16 oz -20 oz glass with ice.
  2. In a small cup or jar stir or shake coffee crystals and hot water, + sweetener (optional).
  3. Pour over ice.
  4. Stir briskly until the ice has melted enough to nearly fill the glass.Add more ice if needed.
  5. Top of with milk andcreamer of choice (optional).
  6. Enjoy
